Sample Email for Consent

Subject: Great Meeting You at [Event Name] – Stay Connected!

Hi [First Name],

It was a pleasure meeting you at [Event Name]. I wanted to follow up 
and share more about how we can help you achieve [specific goal]. 

Would you like to receive updates, tips, and offers via our newsletter? 
Click here to opt-in: [Opt-In Link].

If you’re not interested, feel free to ignore this email, and you won’t 
receive further messages.

Looking forward to staying in touch!

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Your Title/Company Name]  
[Your Contact Information]

Excel Information Fields

Excel Columns to Include:
1. First Name: Contact's first name.
2. Last Name: Contact's last name.
3. Email Address: Contact's email address.
4. Phone Number: Contact's phone number (if applicable).
5. Event Name/Source: Name of the event or source where the contact was met.
6. Consent Status: Mark as "Express" or "Implied."
7. Date Collected: The date when the contact information was collected.
8. Notes: Any additional context or details about the lead.
